grilled cheese sandwiches with bacon and tomato

ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 2 thinly sliced tomato
  • 8 slices of cheese
  • butter

instructions

  1. Cook the bacon: In a skillet over medium heat, cook the bacon until it's crispy. Once cooked, transfer the bacon to a paper towel-lined plate to drain off excess grease.
  2. Prepare the tomatoes: Slice the tomatoes thinly and set them aside.
  3. Assemble the sandwiches: Take two slices of bread for each sandwich. Spread butter or mayonnaise on one side of each slice.
  4. Layer the ingredients: On the non-buttered side of one slice of bread, place a slice of cheese, followed by a layer of cooked bacon, then a few slices of tomato. Top with another slice of cheese and cover with the second slice of bread, buttered side facing outwards.
  5. Grill the sandwiches: Preheat a skillet or griddle over medium heat. Place the assembled sandwiches onto the skillet or griddle. Cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  6. Serve: Once the sandwiches are grilled to perfection, remove them from the heat. Allow them to cool for a minute or two, then slice them diagonally and serve hot.
  7. Enjoy your delicious grilled cheese sandwiches with bacon and tomato! You can pair them with your favorite side dishes or enjoy them on their own.
